Set Number of Teams

Choose how many teams you want (minimum 2, maximum 20). The app will distribute members evenly across all teams.

4

Manual Adjustments (Optional)

After randomization, you can fine-tune teams using drag and drop:

  • Move to Different Team: Drag a person's name to an empty area of another team
  • Swap Positions: Drag one person directly onto another person to swap their teams
